 楚南 李 开 元 校注

奇门遁甲鸣法序 
术数之学，唯奇门一家，非他技可比。征之于数，实出于河图洛书、九宫八卦；征之于理，实系于天地人文、阴阳消息。但历年悠远，真经久佚，犹兼古昔圣王，只传其心，未明发其口；继世贤哲，止著其粗，未笔明其精。言传风后神章，子房秘旨，亦只耳聆其音，未面观其色也。况又杜撰由唐吕，罹火自秦皇，则应前代之成规，荡然绝灭，后世之伪篇，倏忽施张，至业是学者，尊伪说如准绳，习是数者，法谬言似纪纲，以妄导妄，以盲引盲，所以则伪本随时而日兴，真旨随时而淹没矣。
予自弱冠，攻乎是技，遍访明师，博求诸史，购书数十余笈，读止千百卷已，未有不北辙南辕，此臧彼否，换段移条，摘章更旨者也。其规则清纯，理论不杂，从未之有也。以是其志稍有怠。噫！形愈悴，心愈专，道愈迷，力愈进，手不释卷，十数年之鸡窗，寝不就枕；百余月之辛勤，亦良可惜耳！奈心志未甘，苟闻讲究奇门之书者，精研奇门之术者，毋拘险阻，必谒求之。既谒之人，既求之书，亦皆予胸中了了，竟无有超出于某腹外之喜心。
三生有幸，宿世有缘，庚午之岁秋七月，街市偶来一卖卜人邹妙峰者，精于此术，相与友，仅善出只字片言，则较比俗本，大为详确，盖少有矛盾，亦不待辨焉。今祀冬日，俄降天真子孙道一老人，于卜之下，时遭骤雨，高座清淡，老人启悬河之口，开混沌之天，发青囊之秘，倾黄册之玄，句句金销，字字珠钤，思之其胸罗日月，掌握乾坤者欤！想慈颔虎额，龙目鹤姿，伟躯丰颐，隆准庞眉，神光灿灿，苍髯垂垂，形容古峭，貌像异奇，绝非庸常之器，因而拜之，老人婆心，即命润笔，条笺详录，与予曰：峨眉山宏农道人之心印，而口授于我者也，命毋须妄传，今观子形若槁木，心若死灰，芳年未壮而甘隐于卜筮，是必达时知命，修身炼性之人也，故可以授之。戒慎勿遗匪人。幸甚，苟有良善之士，志于斯者，亦不可吝焉。予览其诀，伏迄拜谢，潜心悟之，恍然有得，可谓辨伪于千年之前，指南于百代之后，业古之津梁，奕世之宝筏，渡生出苦海之间，济难离网罗之穴，乃为大有益于天下者。予故不敢自善，亦不敢行欺，谨遵师教，结撰九篇，聊备高明同志云尔。<br> 辽东 龙伏山人
岁次 同治辛未仲冬五日（１８７１年）。


李开元按：序言非常清晰的写出了作者学习奇门遁甲的经历，把其中的苦乐辛酸，描绘得栩栩如生，可谓历历在目。这种没有真传的学习，作者用了上十年时间，废寝忘食，读书无数，都无法入门，可见其难度非同一般。而后来有缘得到孙道人指点，便恍然顿悟！尽悉前疑！肯定任何一个奇门爱好者都有非常深刻的类似于此的感受！然而，奇门真诀，历来都是口传心授，所以知之者甚少。如若习得真诀，也许真可如古人所言：一朝得闻遁甲诀，三界神鬼任驱驰！


<p>释入门飞宫布局之意</p>
<p>阴阳秘旨极玄微，二至分途顺逆飞。</p>
<p>冲和之气，无过乎阴阳；统摄阴阳之理，无过乎节气。故冬至阳生，夏至阴生。阳生则阴消，则万物随之所以生，乃知来之候，故奇门家用之于顺也。阴始则阳息，万物随之所以死，乃数往之候，奇门家用之于逆也。顺逆异途，其星神门仪分而布之，则玄微之旨明矣。</p>
<p>先排掌上九宫位，次按节下三元依。</p>
<p>先于掌上排坎一坤二震三巽四中五乾六兑七艮八离九。次按八节定局数。以五日为一元，尽六甲之数；十五日为三元，尽一气之数。而以甲己加子午卯酉为上元，加寅申巳亥为中元，加辰戌丑未为下元。上元用上局，中元用中局，下元用下局。既而步之，自甲子冠首至癸亥终止，则三元三局自辨矣。</p>
<p>交气正时为心法，折补元局是妙机。</p>
<p>认局之法，当以节候正时为定。而分三局上以时为准。</p>
<p>冬至十二为阳遁，夏至十二阴遁施。</p>
<p>辨却阴阳分局布，六仪三奇依次移。要将六仪值符取，须以八门值使随。</p>
<p>值符加于时干上，值使加之于时支。阳遁顺仪奇逆布，阴遁逆仪奇顺行。</p>
<p>九星顺逆随遁转，八门九星顺宫驰。此象列宿天旋转，九宫无逆是根基。</p>
<p>八门九星飞排定，五随五布向六七。中宫盖皆无值使，值使为五人罕知。</p>
<p>总然八门临在五，何必寄宫坤艮矣。</p>
<p>李开元注：此开篇即言定局、布局、推演之法。根据《皇极经世书》原理，不单有年月日时四家奇门，尚有元会世运奇门，布法皆同。此千古之秘，不露于书，故历代无人知之者。</p>
<p>&nbsp;<wbr></p>
<p>盘盘皆有支神位，甲子起子甲戌施。复自甲而数至癸，位位暗干可知之。</p>
<p>飞支可以知神煞，暗干可以晓公私。神煞公私皆知已，要明星神与门仪。</p>
<p>李开元注：申明暗干飞支的起法、用法。也是遁甲式中千古之秘！得者当秘之！不知暗干飞支，便不明遁甲真机。</p>
<p>&nbsp;<wbr></p>
<p>戊己庚辛壬癸仪，乙丙丁干是三奇，六仪除戊为五凶，三奇总言吉而宜。</p>
<p>甲乙丙丁戊阳长，庚辛壬癸己阴资。盖然戊寄于艮上，己寄于坤是衰时。</p>
<p>六甲隐藏六仪下，遁甲一意从此始。</p>
<p>李开元注：此言奇仪。戊与己那是生杀二气之首领，各统一方。</p>
<p>&nbsp;<wbr></p>
<p>八门休死伤与杜，五开惊生直入景。休生开兮为吉门，伤杜景死惊凶户。</p>
<p>唯有中五无吉凶，逢之仍属没门路。有门有路尚可游，无门无路休展步。</p>
<p>开阴极阳生，休一阳来复，生开阳资物，伤阳泄精华，杜阳极阴始，</p>
<p>景一阴已结，死阳气已消，惊阴作肃杀。</p>
<p>三吉为阳气之动，故吉也。五凶乃阴气之凝，故凶也。五皇极之位，安宁之阙，与百官巡守八方，所以在处即帝座宫辰，而常人遇之非吉之有。盖由无门无路，而步则何由得展也。故以避之为宜。</p>
<p>李开元注：总论八门分九宫之法。中五无门，所到之处皆为闭塞。故曰&#8220;避五&#8221;</p>
<p>&nbsp;<wbr></p>
<p>九星蓬芮冲辅禽，心柱任英有吉凶。蓬芮大凶次英柱，辅禽心星上吉方。</p>
<p>天冲天任小吉路，凶逢生处旺更凶，吉至克宫喜转怒，吉得生乡大吉昌。</p>
<p>凶归弱地凶不顾，凶星失地变吉神。</p>
<p>九星之吉凶，本璇玑与玉衡，但凶星亦有吉时，吉宿亦有凶时。谓其得地失地、生宫克宫是之。假如天蓬临于坤艮宫，受土之克何凶之有？至逢春夏之际，为死囚之气，时值衰替，纵得生扶则害亦难成焉。其吉星之臧否概访诸此耳。</p>
<p>李开元注：此言九星。以旺衰定吉凶，吉星喜旺，凶星喜衰。</p>
<p>&nbsp;<wbr></p>
<p>九神值符腾蛇是，太阴六合勾陈次，太常朱雀九地天，午后白虎玄武治。</p>
<p>值符九天吉且怡，太阴六合九地利，勾陈螣蛇为最凶，白虎玄武乃灾崇。</p>
<p>朱雀旺则小吉云，失地之时转为凶。太常五行化气神，吉凶无应皆不喜。</p>
<p>明得星门神奇仪，格局吉凶要周备。</p>
<p>&nbsp;<wbr>&nbsp;<wbr>&nbsp;<wbr> 九神之吉凶，盖秉八卦之性情而来也。其用勾用虎，用玄用朱，有阴遁阳遁、午前午后之别。并有因事虎、因事用勾，或因事用朱、因事用玄之意哉。在学人按经会悟、依干求支者焉。</p>
<p>李开元注：九神吉凶分论。上篇总论入门布局之法，其中精义，蕴涵遁甲原理，熟读精思便可知悉，非浅识懒散者所能明！起例：天盘奇仪、九门（增一中门）、九星、九神（增一太常）全都按123456789九宫路线飞布。九门阴阳遁均按&#8220;休死伤杜中开惊生景&#8221;顺飞九宫。九星阴遁阳遁均按&#8220;蓬芮冲辅禽心柱任英&#8221;顺飞九宫。九神阳遁按&#8220;符蛇阴合陈常雀地天&#8221;顺飞九宫，阴遁按&#8220;符蛇阴合虎常玄地天&#8221;逆飞九宫。同时在地盘直符布九神阳顺阴逆而飞，一隐一显，一明一暗。星仪门神俱入中五。乃明朝陶仲文真人，从天星奇门古籍《黄石公奇门七十二局军中应验神符经》中提升而出的排局法。在运用中，由于此法尚气运流布，故风水选择、本命改运换运方面优于兵家转盘。</p>
<p>释开端主客四宫之秘</p>
<p>李开元注：此篇极其重要！为奇门遁甲占测之总纲。</p>
<p>欲晓要明开口旨，天盘发用时干已，时干乃是主宰神，格局吉凶考臧否，</p>
<p>吉则吉兮凶则凶，进退存亡泄咫尺。</p>
<p>时干乃一时之主宰，一卦之纲领，事之祸福，为之成败，盖皆前见。而可进可退，当存当亡，无不了然在目。与天乙太乙地乙互相生克、并用神年命较兑其初末之应，休咎之验而全在兹矣。</p>
<p>李开元注：时干乃天机发用之始。故为一数之纲领。</p>
<p>&nbsp;<wbr></p>
<p>次便详察天乙宫，应事以来为初起；太乙人盘事应中，家门吉凶要详细；</p>
<p>地盘地乙末缘由，所主之事是归计。</p>
<p>天乙者，天盘值符也，主事之初应；太乙者，人盘值使也，主事之中应并家内之妍媸；地乙者，地盘值符也，主事之末应。皆以其宫格局吉凶而论之，及参合时干、年命、用神生克以决焉。</p>
<p>李开元注：遁甲三乙诀。定前后始终之状态，非此不能察看。</p>
<p>&nbsp;<wbr></p>
<p>四宫定兮年命推，所卜用神要参契；假如来人问求谋，时干年命定隆替。</p>
<p>四宫者，即天盘时干宫及三乙落宫也。天盘戊字宫，甲年生人亦然。用神者，即求谋以时干宫与年命宫生克决之。余仿此。</p>
<p>年克年命年内凶，月克年命月内厄；日生年命日内昌，时生年命时内美。</p>
<p>年月日时四天干，天盘奇仪九宫旨；与命生克相较看，祸福吉凶可启齿。</p>
<p>卜年命月令日下时间，皆察此四干之宫而与年命之宫生克决矣。</p>
<p>总以生我而为吉，克我吉凶在用矣；我若克他乘问言，他来脱我终非喜。</p>
<p>无论何事，总以他来生我为吉。若来克我，须详所问之事，如我去求他的文书财物，虽属克我，主我之所得必速，则亦不谓之凶；若非此类，其害必速矣。其我克他亦察其问事，若彼人来向我求财求物，而我虽克他，不免与他人之财物；若非此类，其吉可言也。至于我去生他，其中必是耗我之神气，我难获其善，则不可不慎。学者如此决断，则无不中矣。</p>
<p>李开元注：详细论述彼此生克之机，判断吉凶。</p>
<p>&nbsp;<wbr></p>
<p>用主用客非是此，地盘地乙是主司，天盘值符名天乙，九宫以斯为客基；</p>
<p>中人都来观太乙，生克彼我验醇庇。</p>
<p>以地盘值符为主，以天盘值符为客，以人盘值使为中人之偏正。以心会悟，以悟了然，则开口取断，无不立竿见影！</p>
<p>休咎定自天盘时，主客分明虚与实；时生地乙主军利，时生天符客军吉；</p>
<p>若然值使时生兮，中人分外心自逸。其或主客同受生，两造吉凶法归一。</p>
<p>克客盗客客非宜，盗主克主主有失；主客皆克时干宫，此事搅扰无头绪。</p>
<p>主克时干主不静，客克时干客先举；太乙或来克时宫，中人是非无定许。</p>
<p>至若年命与用神，与时生克亦此语。</p>
<p>主客虽定，而时干之宫尤为最要！然时干生克盗泄年命太乙、主客用神，及受年命太乙、主客用神之盗泄生克，则不可不一一详之。</p>
<p>夫时干者，一卦之主宰，万卜之提纲，上帝之座，人王之位也。故民得其庇荫者吉，受其衍责者凶；得其生抚者存而兴，受其克盗者亡而败。时干克我谓之天逆人，我制时干谓之人逆天，安可乎？要人之力以胜天，非德不立。如不修德，而我总以神机巧妙夺其造化，天未必能容之耶？是以告凡者，务主时干，则事之可不可，必昭然而定。苟自负其才以悖天，天理其不遭过衍，则吾不信焉。</p>
<p>李开元注：奇门之中，尤以主客最为紧要。此主客口诀当细心参详。</p>
<p>&nbsp;<wbr></p>
<p>时值天蓬勿问晴，盗贼蜂起喜筑城；天芮多主奸臣起，雾漫天昏乐埋兵；</p>
<p>雷霆震动天冲位，投武雪冤有权衡；访道兴师与修造，辅星所主巽风鸣；</p>
<p>祭祀皇天与埋葬，天禽战胜任纵横；修符合药求神圣，雪雾飘来心有声；</p>
<p>如或天英小雨泽，阴谋固守柱边营；云生气施逢天任，谒贵禳神此处精；</p>
<p>举火问晴兼献计，亢旱推之在天英。</p>
<p>以上九星值时之应，以主天时之事。</p>
<p>李开元注：九星察天时之应。以时干所得之九星定之。</p>
<p>&nbsp;<wbr></p>
<p>九天时值好扬兵，九地潜藏可立营，伏兵且审太阴地，六合交来利埋名，</p>
<p>朱雀之时要调说，火攻腾蛇喜怪魔，勾陈决定遣人捕，白虎交锋死斗戈，</p>
<p>玄武逢之劫寨去，太常以酒迷娄罗，值符最贵招安好，此是九神运用歌。</p>
<p>九神专司地理，故兵家主之。以克时干之值时。</p>
<p>李开元注：九神观地理吉凶，以时干方位定之。</p>
<p>&nbsp;<wbr></p>
<p>干值开门辨官合，求名应试定甲科；休为喜乐和美事，生上耕耘并医瘥；</p>
<p>伤宜捕猎兼讨债，杜好藏形与隐遮，破阵投书须验景，行刑吊死孝边过，</p>
<p>惊能擒讼行欺诈，中五无门宜守窝，八门九路时宫考，祸福无虞可奈何？</p>
<p>八门主人事，乘之时干，而克应可知。</p>
<p>李开元注：八门主人事，亦以时干所临之门而定之。如此天地人三才既备，潜心推测，日久便有灵机。</p>

<p>释占卜用神必观之诀</p>
<p>李开元注：上篇论整体大局，此篇即分事用之不同而分别取用占测之法。一整纲，一分述。其中奥妙，惟可领悟，难以言传！</p>
<p>又有用神法一端，求财谋所生上观，行市降升亦依此。</p>
<p>占求财须观生门宫格局吉凶、与年命宫生克盗泄决之。如生门宫生命克命，其财必得，若命宫克生门生财反是。其行市涨落，只以生门宫格局吉凶、迫制和义及旺衰断之，则不必与年命参矣。占卖卖货物、利息有无、并谋生财之所，亦仿此也。</p>
<p>功名词讼向开干。</p>
<p>如占功名以开门与命宫生克盗泄决之。而词讼胜负，则开门为官星与官长，察其生克主客而断。盖原告为主，被告为客是矣。</p>
<p>捕人讨债都三位，寻物伤门要验看。</p>
<p>捕人以伤门与六合论之。讨债以伤门与天乙论之。寻物以伤门与年命论之是也。</p>
<p>家中否泰查值使。</p>
<p>占家中之吉凶，盖以守门之奇仪为定。即玉女守门、太白入门之类是矣。</p>
<p>六合逃亡定安危。</p>
<p>如走人盖向六合宫觅之，走失亦然。</p>
<p>涉水婚姻休上遇。</p>
<p>占婚姻以休门为喜神。值符为男家，地乙为女家，六合为媒人，男命为男，女命为女，验其生克福庇。总以休门为主决之可耳。涉水行船则年命与休门参焉，详其生克盗泄而祸福可知矣。</p>
<p>潜行避难杜边占。</p>
<p>我要避难潜形，须详杜门之与我生克决之。大约以生我为吉，我生为凶，克我为忧，我克为虞矣。</p>
<p>信息文书推景上，朱雀之位亦相兼。</p>
<p>如问文章之福庇，信音之动止，并有关乎文字之者，全在乎景门宫、朱雀宫之与年命生克旺衰决之。</p>
<p>病症病人之本命，芮星可决生死矣。</p>
<p>占病以病人年命宫与天芮宫、并生死二门宫格局生克参看。其病症之在脏在腑，则以奇仪定之。</p>
<p>口舌斗殴并擒讼，惊门生我定占先。</p>
<p>占口舌斗殴擒讼之事，俱观惊门宫与年命宫格局生克断之。</p>
<p>若欲安居须审五，五宫生命福绵绵。</p>
<p>若欲安居问吉凶，须审中五之宫，若五宫生我比我，必属吉庆。克我不必然矣。何谓也？然五者，居于八宫之中，为皇极之位，极乐之境，虽无门路，但处静修业，得是宫生之，则所以吉矣。</p>
<p>雀噪但详朱雀位。</p>
<p>雀噪，须观朱雀下临之位格局吉凶与命宫生克断之。</p>
<p>禽鸣须按禽之缘。</p>
<p>禽谓凤凰鹰雁鹊鸡鸭鹅鹦鹉之类是也。若此时鹅来而鸣，则按天禽宫格局吉凶与主年命断之。</p>
<p>路途动静决白虎，大小风声此宫研。</p>
<p>欲知道路吉凶，须观白虎宫与命宫生克决之。欲知风之大小，来之方向，亦概详此耳。</p>
<p>交易须先详主客，谋事成败验三传。</p>
<p>交易以出货者为主，纳货者为客，以值使为中人，两家生克定成败。谋事以命宫为主，与时干三乙四宫生克详之，格局吉凶，而存亡可知焉。</p>
<p>雨卜天蓬壬癸地。</p>
<p>问卜有雨无雨，则察天蓬壬癸所落之宫旺衰断矣，及得生得克、临于九宫以参详焉。</p>
<p>田禾要向任星缠。</p>
<p>田禾之收否，须察天任一宫吉凶与太岁宫生克决之，及三乙宫而详初中末也。</p>
<p>英星可以辨晴日。</p>
<p>如问晴否？须看天英之生旺，若英星失陷，须待水神废没之令断之则是。</p>
<p>玄武可知贼后前。</p>
<p>被窃，详玄武宫与命宫生克，及前后可知矣。</p>
<p>大盗须占六庚下。</p>
<p>盗贼虚实去来，皆以天盘六庚入墓空亡断之焉。</p>
<p>攻城要将开字看。</p>
<p>攻城下寨，要决开门。临中五三七之宫断之。</p>
<p>禽宿丙奇为我将，敌宫受制他胆寒。</p>
<p>如丙奇天禽皆为主将，以克制敌宫为宜。</p>
<p>如占国运征其岁，治乱三传亦共恭。</p>
<p>国运以太岁气运之神而详察之也；再以时干三乙四宫生克冲合以参之，而因革治乱，则必遽然而定矣。</p>
<p>动静之间皆可卜，惊闻之内尽须占。</p>
<p>凡我动静之间，忽有所闻，忽有所感，及闻之非常令人诧异而震我心神，见之非常令人惊愕而惑我志意，则皆可以卜之，不必拘拘于有事也。</p>
<p>若问北方人来否，与我相生必至然。</p>
<p>北方之人来与不来，其方与命宫相生、及格局进茹、我和义财必然至矣。</p>
<p>狂风凛冽云出柚，日月交食正时吟。</p>
<p>若或云气非常，风雷奇异，及日食月食，蚩尤彗星、月晕反朝，草木非时，并与人间之罕见，山川之忽崩忽竭，皆以所见之日正时起布局，则祸福休咎必昭然于目也。</p>
<p>李开元注：此论分用占断法。乃奇门占测之精要。言辞简单易懂，无须注释。学者务必熟读精研，方可得其窍要，而后临时占断，如有神助！如此头绪清晰，确切无疑之论，正是奇门古籍中之极品！</p>

释十干生克之正格 </span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: '楷体_GB2312','serif'; mso-bidi-font-weight: bold">李开元注：奇门占法，全以生克旺衰定吉凶。此论十干之生克，乃重中之重。尚有门宫生克，定义和迫制四式；宫间生克，决吉凶悔吝四象。易理玄微，慧心之人自可以一悟百，一通百通。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-weight: bold; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times new roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times new roman'">戊己庚辛壬下癸，进茹臧否喜来前。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: '楷体_GB2312','serif'; mso-bidi-font-weight: bold; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">天盘戊加地盘己，至庚加辛、壬加癸、乙加丙、丁加戊之类，皆谓之进茹格，凡谋为之事，不论吉凶，宜于进步也。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-weight: bold; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times new roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times new roman'">辛庚己戊退茹是，休咎皆宜步回旋。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: '楷体_GB2312','serif'; mso-bidi-font-weight: bold; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">天盘辛加地盘庚，至己加戊、乙加甲、丁加丙之类，皆谓之退茹格，无论吉凶，退步为美。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-weight: bold; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times new roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times new roman'">乙丁壬甲前间格，进步中途有阻隔。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: '楷体_GB2312','serif'; mso-bidi-font-weight: bold; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">天盘乙加地盘丁，至壬加甲、庚加壬、癸加乙之类，谓之前间格，主前进中途有人阻隔之象也。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-weight: bold; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times new roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times new roman'">后间丁乙甲壬类，退后当中犯艰难。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: '楷体_GB2312','serif'; mso-bidi-font-weight: bold; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">天盘丁加地盘乙，至甲加壬、壬加庚、庚加戊之类，谓之后间格，主后退当中有人缠搅之象也。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: '楷体_GB2312','serif'; mso-bidi-font-weight: bold; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">李开元注：奇仪顺逆之进茹退茹诀，言事物之变化，人事之进退。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span lang="EN-US" style="COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: '楷体_GB2312','serif'; mso-bidi-font-weight: bold; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" xml:lang="EN-US">&nbsp;<wbr></span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-weight: bold; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times new roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times new roman'">上合乙庚甲与己，上亲于下下人欢。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: '楷体_GB2312','serif'; mso-bidi-font-weight: bold; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">天盘乙加地盘庚，及甲加己、丙加辛、丁加壬之类，皆谓之上合格，是以上亲下之象，而下人吉耳。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-weight: bold; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times new roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times new roman'">下合壬丁癸加戊，下恭于上上保安。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: '楷体_GB2312','serif'; mso-bidi-font-weight: bold; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">天盘庚加地盘乙，及壬加丁、癸加戊、辛加丙之类，皆谓之下合格，是以下敬上之象，而上者吉也。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-weight: bold; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times new roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times new roman'">合土耕种宜农园。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: '楷体_GB2312','serif'; mso-bidi-font-weight: bold; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">合土格谓甲与己、己与甲是也，宜农园事。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-weight: bold; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times new roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times new roman'">合金钱财宜兵权。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: '楷体_GB2312','serif'; mso-bidi-font-weight: bold; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">合金格谓乙与庚、庚与乙是也，宜钱财威武事。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-weight: bold; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times new roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times new roman'">合水漂泊宜淫私。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: '楷体_GB2312','serif'; mso-bidi-font-weight: bold; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">合水格谓丙加辛、辛加丙是也，宜飘荡淫邪事。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-weight: bold; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times new roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times new roman'">合木发生宜市贾。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: '楷体_GB2312','serif'; mso-bidi-font-weight: bold; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">合木发生格谓丁加壬、壬加丁是也，宜经营动作事。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-weight: bold; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times new roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times new roman'">合火文书与音信，功名筹策宜聘贤。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: '楷体_GB2312','serif'; mso-bidi-font-weight: bold; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">合火格谓戊加癸、癸加戊是也，宜文谋筹策事。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: '楷体_GB2312','serif'; mso-bidi-font-weight: bold; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">李开元注：奇仪合格诀，化合五行为真五行，故应所主事物之宜忌。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span lang="EN-US" style="COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: '楷体_GB2312','serif'; mso-bidi-font-weight: bold; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" xml:lang="EN-US">&nbsp;<wbr></span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-weight: bold; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times new roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times new roman'">正冲庚上忽加甲，对面冲锋毋拘牵。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: '楷体_GB2312','serif'; mso-bidi-font-weight: bold; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">正冲格谓甲与庚、乙与辛、壬与丙、癸与丁是也，宜对面理论、冲锋比武，不可畏惧他人。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-weight: bold; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times new roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times new roman'">背冲甲上庚相见，背地击敌可凯旋。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: '楷体_GB2312','serif'; mso-bidi-font-weight: bold; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">背冲格谓反正冲者是也，宜背地攻伐偷劫之事。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-weight: bold; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times new roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times new roman'">金冲先要扬威武。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: '楷体_GB2312','serif'; mso-bidi-font-weight: bold; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">天盘庚加地盘甲及辛加乙谓之金冲格，宜扬威振武，演阵将兵，争战斗杀之事也。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-weight: bold; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times new roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times new roman'">木冲须宜用义宣。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: '楷体_GB2312','serif'; mso-bidi-font-weight: bold; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">天盘甲加地盘庚及乙加辛谓之木冲格，宜用义宣扬，行谦下士，叶林作伏、滚木作攻之事。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-weight: bold; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times new roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times new roman'">火冲用文及用火。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: '楷体_GB2312','serif'; mso-bidi-font-weight: bold; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">天盘丙加地盘壬及丁加癸谓之火冲格，宜行文招士，立词安民，运筹谋划，欺诈火攻之事也。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-weight: bold; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times new roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times new roman'">水冲行智并行船。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: '楷体_GB2312','serif'; mso-bidi-font-weight: bold; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">天盘壬加地盘丙、及癸加丁谓之水冲格，宜用智行诱，酒食宴乐，调河开渠，水战水攻之事。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-bidi-font-weight: bold; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times new roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times new roman'">甲去冲庚冲属木，庚来冲甲是金关。诸冲尽是此之类，一列其余莫疏顽。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: '楷体_GB2312','serif'; mso-bidi-font-weight: bold; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">此上言冲格之属，以一作例而余可尽知也。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: '楷体_GB2312','serif'; mso-bidi-font-weight: bold; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">李开元注：奇仪冲格诀。并其相应之所主事宜。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span lang="EN-US" style="COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: '楷体_GB2312','serif'; mso-bidi-font-weight: bold; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" xml:lang="EN-US">&nbsp;<wbr></span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times new roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times new roman'">支破即如庚见癸，谋为不就事虚然。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: '楷体_GB2312','serif'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">支破格即庚加癸、癸加庚，壬加己、己加壬，戊加辛、辛加戊是之，主谋为不就，诸事难成，破财荡产之事。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times new roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times new roman'">耗气丙宫加六戊，我之财物不周全。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: '楷体_GB2312','serif'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">丙加戊、戊加庚、庚加壬、壬加甲、甲加丙谓之耗气格，主耗我之元阳，而伤我之财物也。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times new roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times new roman'">夺权如乙加丁位，我之权财被人夺。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: '楷体_GB2312','serif'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">乙加丁、丁加己、己加辛、辛加癸、癸加乙谓之夺权格，被人夺我之权衡也。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times new roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times new roman'">丙加于己交阴象，隐匿阴私利伏藏。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: '楷体_GB2312','serif'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">丙加己、戊加辛、甲加丁、壬加乙、庚加癸谓之交阴格，利于妇女淫邪之事，并有隐匿阴私之象。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times new roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times new roman'">丁加戊位交阳是，脱我精神我有伤。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: '楷体_GB2312','serif'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">丁加戊、己加庚、辛加壬、癸加甲、乙加丙谓之交阳格，事虽有进，未免有伤我之精血也。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times new roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times new roman'">庚加六己为得母，家中喜悦庆嘉祥。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: '楷体_GB2312','serif'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">庚加己、戊加丁、壬加辛、丙加乙、甲加癸谓之得母格，事虽属退，然家中之和顺，大人之庇荫，亦必所不免矣。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times new roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times new roman'">己加六丙谓获父，福德贵人在身旁。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: '楷体_GB2312','serif'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">己加丙、辛加戊、癸加庚、乙加壬、丁加甲谓之获父格，主得大人之助，父老之护。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times new roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times new roman'">庚加戊字乘权格，假势兴隆大吉昌。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: '楷体_GB2312','serif'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">庚加戊、戊加丙、丙加甲、甲加壬、壬加庚谓之乘权格，虽属后间，但以进步而扬威之也。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: '楷体_GB2312','serif'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">夫甲隐戊宫，何由辨之？甲加壬、丙加甲也。然则甲加壬非戊加壬，丙加甲非丙加戊，是以辨之。所以六甲之时，用甲之事。及占人之年命属甲者，盖以甲谓之也。如非甲之事（时），而人之年命又不属甲，岂有作甲之论乎？所以易曰：神而明之，存乎其人。此之谓也。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times new roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times new roman'">丁加于乙是倚势，赖恃他人我得扬。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: '楷体_GB2312','serif'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">丁加乙、乙加癸、癸加辛、辛加己、己加丁谓之倚势格，主赖他人制辖家里纷扰，而我之志不能升也。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times new roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times new roman'">乙加己位为外乱，他国来兵要侵疆。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: '楷体_GB2312','serif'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">乙加己、己加癸、癸加丁、丁加辛、辛加乙谓之外乱格，主他国招兵，外人结党，欲凌我边疆之意也。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times new roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times new roman'">己加乙字内人乱，家里纷纷无主张。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: '楷体_GB2312','serif'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">己加乙、乙加辛、辛加丁、丁加癸、癸加己谓之内乱格，主内人纷纷不受法度，而欲作乱也。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: '楷体_GB2312','serif'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">李开元注：夫阳受阴克为害，阴受阳克为侵，阳受阳克为制，阴受阴克为乱。此所以明生克之奥焉。其耗气、夺权、得母、获父等等之格，亦莫不法是矣。盖阳者，刚健之气所成，阴者，柔弱之气所化。寓于人，凡刚而中正为君子，柔而淫私为小人。是故君子制人莫不以法度，小人制人莫不以偏邪。斯格之定，可谓高诸奇门家之论，诚得其真矣。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span lang="EN-US" style="COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: '楷体_GB2312','serif'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" xml:lang="EN-US">&nbsp;<wbr></span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times new roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times new roman'">又有一般入墓诀，学人不可不直说，即如丙丁加乾六，甲乙二宫坤墓绝，</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times new roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times new roman'">癸壬在四辛八艮，戊己亦同丙丁穴。诸事逢之多蹇滞，病人亡命气已绝。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: '楷体_GB2312','serif'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">入墓一格，大为不详，诸事逢之多遭蹇滞，病人死亡，官讼被狱而无出头之象也。必待冲墓之年月或日时始可以得好矣。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: '楷体_GB2312','serif'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">李开元注：入墓诀。此为五行真墓，非干墓。真墓较干墓尤凶。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span lang="EN-US" style="COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: '楷体_GB2312','serif'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt" xml:lang="EN-US">&nbsp;<wbr></span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times new roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times new roman'">甲子旬中空甲戌，甲戌旬中甲申庚，甲申原来空甲午，甲午辛符辰壬空，</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times new roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times new roman'">甲辰空为甲寅癸，甲寅旬中甲子空。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: '楷体_GB2312','serif'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">然旬空之宫，纵生扶主宫命宫，亦属无益也。逢空，吉凶不成之象，即值符前逆也。逢之须待出空之日期也，方可决其事可否为之成败也。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times new roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times new roman'">主空主人心虚诈。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: '楷体_GB2312','serif'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">主即地乙也，主空即戊上乘己也。主乃主人也，主人虚诈不实也。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times new roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times new roman'">天乙乘空名没首，谋为一切始难通。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: '楷体_GB2312','serif'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">天乙乘空名没首格，主初事不利，斩头之象也。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times new roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times new roman'">值使落空名失中，半途而废枉用功。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: '楷体_GB2312','serif'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">值使落空名失中，前功尽弃，断腰之象。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times new roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times new roman'">年命空亡自虚惊。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: '楷体_GB2312','serif'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">年命乘空主虚惊之象，心神不定之意，主进退不决。占病近则吉，久病则凶。芮星空多吉。占事必待出空之日方可成也。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times new roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times new roman'">用神若空求难遂，为义反复犯叮咛。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: '楷体_GB2312','serif'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">用神即为求财则必看生门是也。如空难求财，而不能有，而难如意。仿此。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times new roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times new roman'">格中纵然逢生命，命空徒然一场空，逢空纵然无始终。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: '楷体_GB2312','serif'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">旬空之宫虽生命亦无益也。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times new roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times new roman'">天乙若然值旬空，濡首之格事无终。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: '楷体_GB2312','serif'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">天乙落空，事之无终归著也。名濡首格，截足之象。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 24pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="FONT-SIZE: 12pt; COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times new roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times new roman'">天时若空名无绪，任君鸿谋亦难行。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: '楷体_GB2312','serif'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">以天盘时干乃一卦之主宰，不宜旬空。如空，名无绪格，主妙策难行之象。</span></p>
<p style="TEXT-INDENT: 21pt; LINE-HEIGHT: 20pt; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0; mso-line-height-rule: exactly"><span style="COLOR: black; FONT-FAMILY: '楷体_GB2312','serif'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">李开元注：空亡诀。诀中玄微，实他书所未及者。</span></p>
